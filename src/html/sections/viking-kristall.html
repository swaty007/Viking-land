Viking Kristall was established by Stig Nielsen in the late 80s and has grown to be a significant player in the industry. Nowadays they work with European museums and designers and their jewellery is exported to more than ten countries. They are passionate about their Nordic heritage, and using original, powerful Viking design and craftsmanship to inspire new jewellery.

Like the Vikings, Viking Kristall work in silver, bronze and gold, and only use genuine stones and crystals in their jewellery. Their silversmiths have mastered the exact techniques employed a thousand years ago.

After centuries in the grave, the Viking aesthetic has been brought back to life. Viking design still captivates people today. Ancient symbols have a timeless appeal. Viking Kristall take pride in giving new life to the Nordic Viking heritage.
