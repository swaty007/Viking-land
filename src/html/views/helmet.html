With unique combination of classic cinematic storytelling and innovative 270- degree filmmaking, we are inviting the audience to step into the Viking times. Inside a seven by eight meter Viking helmet theatre and with a screen spanning the entire volume of the interior space, various memorable moments from daily life more than a thousand years ago, are unfolding either as full 270 immersive and VR like immersion - or as a mosaic of multiple cinema images playing simultaneously, accompanied by a soundtrack and atmospheric soundscapes.

This epic 14 minutes film runs four times an hour continuously as an installation where the audience can enter and exit the venue freely.
